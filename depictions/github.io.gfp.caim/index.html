Caim ports the iOS 14 pin messages feature to iOS 13.

It's finally here, the tweak that I've been working on day in and day out with passion. One of the best features of iOS 14 is in my opinion is the message pining feature. iOS 14 is exciting, but after about two days on the beta, you'll miss your jailbreak again. That is why I made Caim, to be able to enjoy it, while still being jailbroken.

Caim is built after iOS 14's clean design, not just the looks, but also the feeling. My aim with this tweak is to get it as close to iOS 14 as possible and make your friends wonder how you got the pin feature on iOS 13.

One difference is that you can pin how many conversations that you want compared to iOS 14 which only lets you pin 9 conversations.

I will continue to update this package to tweak small things here and there to get it closer and closer to iOS 14.

How does it work? There's two ways to pin a conversation, 1st one is to drag on the conversation and press pin, 2nd one is to tap the tree dots in the top right corner and then tap on pin. You can in remove pined conversation in this mode as well as pin new conversations.

Compatibility Caim has been extensively tested (hence the release delay) to work on both iPads and iPhones. It also works in lanscape mode, and different layouts in the messages application has been accounted for. It has only been tested for iOS 13. It supports multiple languages as well!

Thanks to @acarbone_0314 for the banner picture and being very helpful with testing, also thanks to @QuixThe2nd for testing the tweak and being helpful. Both these guys are really cool dudes.
