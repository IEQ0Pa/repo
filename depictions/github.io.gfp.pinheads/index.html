No need to wait for iOS 14! Pin favorite chats in Messages application on iOS 13 right now.

Just open messages application after installation and swipe to right on you favorite conversation.

Top Features:

  • Unlimited number of pins
  • Actions to open conversation or unpin from top
  • Integrated with iOS 13 and swipe action

Upcoming Features:

  • Call with your favorite contact from bin bar
  • Add to pin bar from inside of a conversation

  • You can now choose number of columns in vertical layout, between 3, 4 and 5
  • Choose if you want heads to be big (like iOS 14) or small (like early versions of PinHeads) in vertical layout!
