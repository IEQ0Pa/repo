Chroma
Themes

Chroma is a vivid, gradient theme that maintains a feel that is familiar to the stock iOS icons. All the icons are precisely made with a uniform design language throughout.

Chroma currently has over 400 icons, over 30 alternate icons, and 2 icon masks. Additionally, Chroma themes the settings icons and icon badges, creating a consistent experience and appearance throughout the device.
