Rapid
Tweaks
Inspired by iOS 14 layouts and macOS Big Sur, Rapid bring various information, shortcuts to iCloud, Apps, Music, screenshot or lockscreen timer to your device. I have implemented cool feature called Showcase, I know you guys love to screenshot your themes or tweaks and post on Twitter or Reddit to show some cool themes but in order to do that, you will need to use an app or software on pc to add the screenshot on device bezel but this is the first tweak that let you to add some screenshot to device bezel, background image, rotate in 3D, add some texts or drawing tools and accessible anywhere on SpringBoard or in Apps without leaving the Apps then save your showcase to photo library or share them directly to Twitter. You can watch promo video here.

Rapid Features

  • Shortcut to iCloud page in settings, you can add your own avatar and set the name
  • Set the timer countdown to take screenshot automatically (In seconds format)
  • Set the timer countdown to lock your device automatically (In seconds format)
  • Add URL shortcuts take you to specify page on Reddit or Twitter
  • Information about your WiFi and Cellular network and take you to WiFi/Cellular section in settings when tapped
  • 6 Shortcuts to your favourite apps
  • Music album background image on music panel and wave animation when playing, shortcuts to Music or Spotify.
  • Access to showcase editor

Showcase features

  • Choose your preferable color device bezel either notch or legacy (Please note if your screenshot are from legacy device then please use legacy bezel)
  • Add screenshot image
  • Add background image or background color
  • Drawing tools (pen, rectangle, circle, arrow, text)
  • 3D rotation x, y, z axis (Please note, due flat image the device bezel won't actually look 3D)
  • Translation x, y, z axis
  • Scale x, y, z axis
  • Save to your photo library
  • Share sheet to Twitter, messages, notes or whichever apps you want to share

Compatible

  • iOS 13 (Tested on iOS 13.3 and 13.5 I haven't test it on 13.5.1 or betas)
  • iPhone 6S, 6S Plus, 7, 7 Plus, 8, 8 Plus, X, XS, XS Max, XR, 11, 11 Pro, 11 Pro Max and SE (2nd Generation)

Incompatible

  • iOS 12 and below
  • iPad
  • iPhone SE 1st generation
