Spear
Tweaks

Ever get tired of keep swiping up to kill your apps? You have come to the right place, with Spear you can kill apps with one click on Spear button. If you don't want to kill your favourite apps, don't worry I got you covered, you can blacklist your favourite apps so it won't get killed with Spear. Give your apps switcher a modern and sleek look, you can add the time/date to top right/left corner, hide app icons and labels, scale the app cards and quick access to some toggles while in app switcher. Special thanks to CodeOne88 for the concept design, originally named (Closius). Spear won't work if you have grid switchers tweak. Make sure you check the features and compatible list below.

Features

  • Kill apps with one click
  • Blacklist your favourite apps
  • Quick access to some toggle modules (Please note this is not control center tweak)
  • Add time/date to top left/right corner
  • Color time/date
  • Color app cards label
  • Hide app cards icons/label
  • Scale app cards size
  • Work with system light/dark mode
  • You can customize Spear color to your liking
  • Maybe more features coming soon

Compatible

  • iOS 13 only (Tested on iOS 13.5)
  • iPhone only - Please note it's not compatible with iPhone SE 1st generation.

Incompatible

  • iOS 12
  • iPhone SE 1st generation
  • Grid switchers
  • Added 12 hours clock format - requested by users.
