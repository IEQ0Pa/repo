Comet
Tweaks

Inspired by iOS 14 widgets called smart stacks. If you are like me, love iOS 14 widgets on SpringBoard and can't wait for public release of iOS 14 on this fall and unsure whether to update and lose jailbreak or stay on current firmware while jailbroken, looking for something like iOS 14 widgets style and you have come to the right place. Comet bring iOS 14 widgets as alternative to your device, it's not available on SpringBoard but accessible anywhere by using activator gesture and you can access to it on lockscreen, springboard and in applications. You won't need to make a choice which single widgets you want to use, you can have it all in one place, scrolling modules and draggable. Please note it's not actual widget like today view on springboard or iOS 14 widgets where you can add stocks widgets or third party too and MAKE SURE to check the compatibility and dependencies before purchasing comet. Take a look at the promo video here.

Comet Features

  • Comes with 14 modules (Storage, Carrier, Clock, Safari, Maps, Weather, WiFi, Compass, Music, Notes, iCloud, Favourite Apps, Calendar and Battery) Compatible
  • Each modules are tappable to open individual apps
  • Draggable
  • Scrolling in horizional or vertical directions
  • Comes with two styles rounded or leaf
  • Works with system light/dark mode or customise color to your liking
  • Lock button to prevent comet from moving it's position
  • Reorder modules
  • Use activator gesture to invoke or hide comet anywhere

Dependencies

  • AppList from BigBoss repo
  • libimagepicker from Packix repo
  • alderis color picker from Chariz repo
  • Cephei from Chariz repo
  • Activator from BigBoss repo (You will need to have it in order to invoke comet)

Compatible iOS 13 (Tested on iOS 13.3 and 13.5 I haven't test it on 13.5.1 or betas and above) iPhone 6S, 6S Plus, 7, 7 Plus, 8, 8 Plus, X, XS, XS Max, XR, 11, 11 Pro, 11 Pro Max and SE (1st & 2nd Generation)
