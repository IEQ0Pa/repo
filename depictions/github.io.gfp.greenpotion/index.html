GreenPotion
Tweaks

If you don't see the video, please try with Zebra

.
With GreenPotion you can show your battery level in your LockScreen with a nice "potion" animation.
Show the level with the option to speak the percentage in your current language, using the ampoule in the lockscreen or long-press and release the power button, even if the screen is off (it turn on when you're doing the long-press-and-release on power-button)
Preferences:

  • Enable/disable the tweak without respring
  • Speak the battery level in your language
  • Give pleasant vibration feedback
  • Animate the "potion" level
  • Set the speed of the animation
  • Show/hide the ampoule potion in lockscreen
  • Set the ampoule size
  • Use the Power button instead the bottle potion (long-press-and-release)
  • Enable/disable the battery power safe (LPM) with a long-press-and-release the potion bottle in the LockScreen

Remember to long-press the options in the settings page, to have a small hint on every option.
Tested on iOS 13/13.5 in different devices Compatible with "Info 64"
Please remember to update your Cephei to "Cephei Tweak Support" to minimum version of 1.15.1 from Chariz repo.

  • Added complete descriptions to the settings page
  • Decreased the time (to 500 ms) for the long-press in the preferences.
